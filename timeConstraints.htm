Chance to get a maximum of 12 months of OPT per higher degree level OPT must be used during or immediately following completion of a course of study Students in government-designated fields with Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) may be eligible for an additional 17 months of “STEM OPT”.
What are the eligibility requirements for the 17-month extension of post-completion OPT?
  • The student must have a degree included in the STEM Designated Degree Program List.
  • The student must currently be in an approved post-completion OPT period based on a designated STEM degree.
  • The student’s employer must be enrolled in E-Verify.
  • The student must apply on time (i.e., before the current post-completion OPT expires).
