Optional Practical Training (OPT) Definition:
  • must be in the field of student’s major
  • part-time or full-time
  • paid or unpaid
  • may work for more than one employer
Periods of pre-completion OPT are deducted from the available periods of post-completion OPT. OPT is professional employment in your field at a level commensurate with your level of study.
Example: master’s candidate in computer science seeking work in the field of computer science that requires graduate-level training Purpose of the tutorial: retaining permission to work in the USA for one year after the completion of your studies. If you have questions after viewing this tutorial please contact the ISSS office.
