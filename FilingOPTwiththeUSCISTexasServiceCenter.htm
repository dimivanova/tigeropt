ISSS does not recommend filing OPT application on-line, through the process would ideally be simple from our experience it complicates and delays processing ISSS staff will make an electronic scan of the entire OPT packet A scan will be emailed to you for record keeping purposes A scan will be kept on file with the ISSS for our record keeping purposes You will get an original Form I-20 with OPT recommendation and dates. This is your new immigration document, be sure to use it when re-entering the US while on OPT. It is advisable to carry prior originals, especially with Curricular Practical Training (CPT) approvals (if applicable). Make a copy of your complete application before you mail it to USCIS Tracking: complete form G-1145 to receive an e-mail or text notification from USCIS to confirm receipt of your application. This should be included in your OPT application
