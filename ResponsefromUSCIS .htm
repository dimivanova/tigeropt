USCIS will send a notice for the receipt of your I-765 EAD (OPT) application (2-3 weeks) to ISSS office address. Once we receive receipt notice, we will send you a scan so you can track the status of your individual case using the case status search feature of the USCIS Web site. You may elect to receive email alerts about your case status from this Web site. You should receive your employment authorization document (EAD) in three months You may not work before that Check the card for errors as soon as you receive it. A replacement EAD card may be needed if EAD card is lost, stolen or mutilated.
