OPT is recommended by the OISS and authorized by the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) with the issuance of an Employment Authorization Document (EAD) The earliest a student may apply for post-completion OPT is 90-120 days before completion date The OISS strongly recommends students apply as early as possible due to USCIS processing time. It is advisable to start putting your OPT application together at the start of Fall or Spring semesters. Apply no later than 4 weeks after the completion date to allow for adequate processing time. Application for OPT is a 2-step process
