A benefit of F-1 status The rules governing the stay are not the same as those for students still enrolled How to maintain the OPT status? 1. Engage in OPT appropriate volunteer activity of at least 20 hours per week or professional employment that meets the OPT definition 2. Avoid being unemployed or not engaged in volunteer activity for 90 days or longer, as this is considered a violation of status by the Department of Homeland Security 3. Keep a personal record of any and all OPT activity 4. Notify the OISS within 10 days of any of the following: Change in legal name Change in mailing or residential address Change in employer Deciding to depart the US Return to school full time Change of status Any interruption to OPT activity
